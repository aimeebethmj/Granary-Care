    What We Do

    Here at Granary Care, providing excellent childcare has been our greatest focus since we founded 11 years ago.

    We come from a child centered approach that begins with compassion and understanding, seeing the world through their lens and always striving to help them flourish.

    Safety is the number one priority for our experienced team of dedicated staff. With this a given, together we build confidence, we have fun and we create meaningful connections and experiences.

    The three pillars of our services are Afterschool care, our London Nanny Agency and our Mother and Baby Unit.

    Through these, we work towards family wellbeing and family cohesion.

    Giving Back to Good Causes

    Many years ago we learned about the incredible work of the Kondanani Orphange. Inspired by positive change they created, we promised ourselves we would give back to good causes.

    As we grow, we can increase our monetary aid to support the Charities we sponsor.
