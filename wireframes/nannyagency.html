Child Care and Child Development

London Nanny Agency

Here at Granary Care's Nanny Agency we offer a tailormade service to meet the recruitment needs of nurseries, families and childcare providers nationwide.

We take the time to personally meet and get and to know you and your requirements, so we can tailor our services to match your individual needs.

Renowned for our high standards, CRB checks, strict vetting procedure and high calibre of candidates, we take the weight off your shoulders in finding the right match.

We understand the vast benefits of giving your child one-to-one time with a reliable childcare professional.

For us, creating continuous and nurturing relationships is paramount.

At Granary Care, we are constantly working towards family wellbeing and family cohesion.
